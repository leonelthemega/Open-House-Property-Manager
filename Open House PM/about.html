Who we are?

SlateHouse Group Property Management is a successful property management company because we started as landlords of our own investments, just like all of our owners. We are now the property manager for hundreds of properties throughout Philadelphia, Lancaster, Lebanon, York, Reading, Harrisburg, Lewistown, Pottstown, Coatesville, Trenton, Scranton, Allentown, Baltimore, Norfolk, and the surrounding counties.

Maintenance

Marketing and Advertisement

We have licensed technology that allows us to advertise your listing on over 100 websites like; Zillow, Craiglist, Trulia, Rent.com, etc.

Tenant Screaning

OHG performs rigorous background checks on all of our prospective tenants. We are so confident in our screening process that we guarantee it.

Inspection

Apartment inspection are completed regularly to insure tenants are treating your property right. And we do a detailed report to the owner monthly.

Evictions

In circumstances where a tenant breaks the lease, SlateHouse will file all necessary paperwork and represent the owner in court at no extra cost to you (minus court costs). No more legal costs, or headaches of trying to run to a courthouse in the middle of the day.

Move-in

SlateHouse handles entire move-in process for tenant to make it as easy as possible for tenant. We Guarantee our service. If for some reason our tenant is in default of the lease, we will assist you in the eviction process and find you a new tenant at no additional cost.

24/7 Tenant Hotline

We are available to take emergency maintenance calls 24 hours a day. Christmas Day emergency --- tenant calls our emergency hotline and we take care of the issue for you.
