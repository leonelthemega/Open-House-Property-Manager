Complexes Currently Managed by OpenHouse Group

Roebling Lofts - Trenton, NJ

NJ's best and newest upscale loft-style apartments. Roebling Lofts offers astonishing, green-design lofts and abundant apartment and community amenities. These brand new, light-filled apartments are in the original Roebling & Sons factory, rich in history, with unique high-design architectural details, and located in a gritty, diverse, and welcoming neighborhood.

Lofts at Savoy - Elizabethtown, PA

Originally a shoe factory, this large factory was converted into 49 loft style apartments with varying layouts.

Flory Mill - Lancaster, PA

Flory Mill is a mix of both commercial office spaces and apartments. The building dates back to the 1700's when it operated as mill.

Wynfield Apartments - Hatboro, PA

Wynfield Apartments is a 58 Unit Complex in Hatboro — a mix of new town-homes and well maintained two-bedroom apartments.

Longview - Annville, PA

This community was originally built and known as Long Apartments. In 2017 new ownership was established and extensive remodeling was completed. This community boosts over 3 acres of open common areas. Equally impressive are the views of open countryside with the Appalachian Mountains in the distance.

Willow Ridge Manor - Willow Street, PA

Willow Ridge Manor is an upscale townhome community within the Lampeter-Strasburg School District. There are 29 townhomes in this community and all feature 3 bedrooms and 2 full baths. All townhomes have similar layouts but vary in total square footage finishes.

Villas at Willow Run - Willow Street, PA

Willow Run is a new construction community consisting of 72 upscale town homes in a desirable Lancaster County location.

Pennside Court Apartments - Mt Penn, PA

French Quarter Charm! Rent a one-bedroom apartment in a quiet and convenient French Quarter inspired building located 10 miles away from Center City Reading. Newly installed washers and dryers on premise and plenty of amazing amenities within walking distance.
