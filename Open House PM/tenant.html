The Application Proccess

Are you interested in one of our rental properties? Here is what you need to know before renting

Read More

1. Make Sure You Qualify

Pre-Tax income must be 3 times the rent.
Credit scores over 600.
No negative criteria such as past due utilities.
No previous Evictions

2. Schecdule a Showing

Schedule online for best results,
Or Call the Office between 8:30am-6pm M-F. 1-800-837-0037

3. Apply Online

$30 per applicant. All occupants over the age of 18 must apply. This fee is non refundable

4. Get Approved and Pay a Deposit

The property manager will contact you within 2-3 business days and let you know whether you are approved or not
The property manager will email a digital lease that you can click and sign.

5. Other Information

Pet Policies - Most of our rental properties are pet friendly.
Pet Fees - We charge a $300 non-refundable pet fee at move-in and an additional $30 pet fee per month per pet.
